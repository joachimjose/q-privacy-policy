Privacy Policy

How Q Ai handles your data — plainly stated.

Who We Are

Q Ai is an iOS application that converts PDF documents into interactive multiple-choice quizzes using AI. The app is developed and maintained by Jackson Mzimbe ("we", "us", or "our").

Data We Collect

We collect the minimum possible data to operate the app. Specifically:

  • PDF content — files you upload are sent to an AI model to generate quiz questions. We do not store your PDFs on our servers after processing.
  • Quiz data & scores — stored locally on your device using SQLite. We do not sync this to any cloud service.
  • Purchase information — handled entirely by RevenueCat and Apple. We receive only anonymised subscription status; we never see your payment details.
  • Anonymous device identifier — a randomly generated ID stored on your device, used solely to associate likes and bookmarks with your session. It is never linked to your identity.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos (beyond PDFs you explicitly select)
  • Browsing or usage analytics beyond crash reports

Third-Party Services

Q Ai uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • OpenAI / AI provider — processes PDF text to generate quiz questions. Data is subject to their usage policies.
  • RevenueCat — manages in-app subscriptions. See revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Apple App Store — handles all payment processing.

Data Retention & Deletion

All quiz data and scores live exclusively on your device. You can erase all data at any time from Settings → Erase All Data within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Q Ai is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
