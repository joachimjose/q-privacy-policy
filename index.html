Privacy Policy

How Quizly handles your data — plainly stated.

Who We Are

Quizly is an iOS application that converts PDF documents into interactive multiple-choice quizzes using AI. The app is developed and operated by the Quizly team ("we", "us", or "our").

Data We Collect

We collect the minimum possible data required to operate the app. Specifically:

  • PDF content — files you upload are processed by our AI engine to generate quiz questions. We do not store your PDFs on our servers after processing is complete.
  • Quiz data & scores — stored locally on your device. We do not sync this to any external cloud service.
  • Purchase information — handled entirely by Apple and our subscription management provider. We receive only an anonymised subscription status indicator; we never see your payment details.
  • Anonymous device identifier — a randomly generated ID stored on your device, used solely to track your free trial usage and associate app preferences with your session. It is never linked to your personal identity.

Free Trial Data

Quizly offers new users one complimentary quiz generation to experience the app before subscribing. We record whether this trial has been used via an anonymous device identifier stored locally on your device. No personal information is collected or transmitted in connection with the free trial.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos (beyond PDFs you explicitly select)
  • Browsing history or behavioural analytics beyond crash reports

Third-Party Services

Quizly integrates with the following categories of third-party service, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • AI processing — your uploaded PDF text is processed by our AI engine to generate quiz content. The content is used solely for this purpose and is subject to the data handling practices of the underlying AI infrastructure.
  • Subscription management — our subscription provider manages in-app purchase status and renewal. We receive only anonymised entitlement data.
  • Apple App Store — handles all payment processing in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention & Deletion

All quiz data and scores reside exclusively on your device. You can erase all locally stored data at any time from Settings → Erase All Data within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data automatically.

Children's Privacy

Quizly is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app following any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
